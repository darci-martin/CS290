FAQ


Do I need to register to attend?

No, the events are free and open to the public. It is not necessary to pre-register.

Can I sell my own items at the event?

Yes, please refer to the Vendor page for more informtion.

Can children attend?

Yes! Of course! We are a wholesome event for the whole family. Please share your love of videogames with your children.

What kinds of items are for sale?

Our vendors have games, game consoles and accessories from the 80's to today. Products range from new-in-box to used conditions.

How much do items cost?

Pricing is dependent on condition and market rate as comparable to eBay. You can find games for $5 and consoles for $200 and everything in between. Our vendors offer competitive pricing on rare and hard-to-find items, and negotiation is encouraged.
